A bit about me

Most of the time, you'll find me making software and hardware projects. I mainly work with Python, Java, and the web languages (HTML, CSS, JS) but I love learning new things to finish my projects. I also enjoy taking photographs and making music.

I'm currently 17 years old and I attend Sir John A. Macdonald S.S. in Waterloo, Ontario. I am pursuing a career in Computer Science and my goal is to invent something cool and impactful in the future.
